# The Rise of Quantum Encryption Threats

As quantum computing power scales exponentially, legacy cryptographic standards face imminent obsolescence. We are analyzing a surge in "Store Now, Decrypt Later" traffic across backbone network nodes. 

## Key Vulnerabilities
- RSA-2048 and ECC configurations are heavily targeted.
- Government databases utilizing outdated physical layer security.

The immediate transition to Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) is no longer a theoretical debate but an operational imperative.
